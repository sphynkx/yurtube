About

YurTube (YT) is a personal video sharing service hosted at sphynkx.org.ua. You can create a local account or sign in via Google (SSO). Additional providers (GitHub, Discord) may be added later.

Data we use

  • Email (for sign-in and account notifications)
  • Display name and avatar (when you sign in with Google)
  • Local username (unique handle used in URLs and mentions)
  • Uploaded videos and related metadata

How we use it

Only to operate your account (authentication, profile display, content management) and improve the service. We do not sell data and do not share it with third parties other than infrastructure providers required to run the service (hosting/CDN).
